WHY WEVOX EXISTS

Traditional media overlooks youth perspectives.

India has over 50,000 student journalists with stories worth telling — but no platform built for them. WEVOX fills that gap. We are not a news channel. We are not a blog. We are a verified network of student reporters publishing real ground-level stories, field videos, and investigative reports — directly from campuses, communities, and conversations that the mainstream missed.

EDITORIAL STANDARDS

How we verify stories.

01
Verified Student Identity

Every reporter on WEVOX is verified via college ID before publishing. No anonymous submissions.

02
Editorial Review

All stories go through an editorial check for accuracy, fairness, and journalistic standards before going live.

03
No Sponsored Content

WEVOX does not accept paid stories or sponsored journalism. Every story published is 100% editorially independent.
